Help & Support

Welcome

Welcome to the Help & Support page. Here you can find useful information about how to use the dental clinic system and important instructions for patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment from the Booking Appointment page by selecting a doctor, date, and available time slot.
How can I view my prescriptions?
You can check all your prescriptions from the Prescriptions page after logging in.
How can I update my profile information?
You can update your personal details from the Profile page and then click Save Changes.
What should I do if I forget my password?
Please contact the clinic administrator or support team for assistance.

Patient Tips

Tip 1: Please arrive at least 10 minutes before your appointment time.
Tip 2: Make sure your profile information is correct and updated.
Tip 3: Inform the clinic in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
Tip 4: Follow the doctor’s prescription instructions carefully.

Clinic Policy

Policy 1: Appointments should be booked in advance through the system.
Policy 2: Patients must provide correct personal information.
Policy 3: Missed appointments may require rescheduling.
Policy 4: Prescriptions are issued only by authorized doctors.
